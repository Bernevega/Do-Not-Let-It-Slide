# Do Not Let It Slide!

Do Not Let It Slide! is my näyttö (final assignment) project, which I completed in around three weeks in 2023. Since the theme was completely open, I decided to challenge myself by creating a 3D game, as I usually make 2D ones.

The main idea behind the project was to recreate New York City inside Unity by combining several different tools and software. To build the map, I used Google Chrome, Google Earth, RenderDoc, MapsModelImporter, and Blender. After that, I imported everything into Unity, adjusted and edited the environment, and then designed the gameplay around it.

For the visual style, I chose a pixelated look to capture the feeling of old-school games. Because the map capture process with RenderDoc is far from perfect, I decided not to fix every small flaw or hole. Instead, I embraced those imperfections as part of the game’s charm. I wanted the experience to feel nostalgic – like older games that were sometimes rough around the edges, but still fun and memorable.

The goal of the game is to collect as many coins as possible before the timer runs out. The player can jump twice by pressing Space, and when colliding with walls, the character begins to slide and gain speed.
